Common Questions

Everything you need to know

What items do you accept?

I accept a wide variety of items including clothing, electronics, collectibles, home goods, and more. If you're unsure, just describe your items in the application and I'll let you know!

How do I get my items to you?

Depending on your location, you can ship items to me or arrange a local drop-off. I'll provide all the details after reviewing your application.

When do I get paid?

You'll receive payment within 3-5 business days after your item sells and the buyer confirms receipt. Payment is sent via your preferred method.

What about platform fees?

E-commerce platforms like eBay charge 8-13% in fees. These are deducted from the sale price first, then we split the remaining amount 70/30 in your favor.
